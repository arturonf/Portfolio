Thesis: A Grasp-Tabu Heuristic for the Handover Minimization Problem

May 2020

This thesis proposes a hybrid GRASP-Tabu heuristic that finds feasible solutions for the NP Hard Capacitaded Clustering Problem (CCP). The problem fucusses on minimazing the handover in telecomunications grids.
