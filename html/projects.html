Projects

Thesis: A Grasp-Tabu Heuristic for the Handover Minimization Problem

May 2020

This thesis proposes a hybrid GRASP-Tabu heuristic that finds feasible solutions for the NP Hard Capacitaded Clustering Problem (CCP). The problem fucus on minimazing the handover in telecomunications grids.

Electric Scooter

May 2019

Final joint project for electrical machines and power electronics subjects in which we designed and built both the mechanical and electrical parts of an electric scooter with velocity control using a boost converter and a 500W dc Motor.

Line Follower with PID

Nov 2018

Final Control System's Project. We designed a line follower with 8 sensons and PID controll for competions. PID (proportional integral derivative) controllers use a control loop feedback mechanism to control process variables and are the most accurate and stable controller.

XXIX International Industrial and Logistics Engineering Congress

Apr 2018

Required activity for the student council. Being the event's coordinator in the Directive Board of Indistrial Engineering, I was in charge of the panning and logistics involved in the XXIX International Industrial and Logistics Engineering Congress hosted at UDLAP. We had 3 days of lectures and workshops about different relevant topics and 5 trips to plants such as Bimbo, Yakult, Schaeffler, BBVA and Stanley.

Robotic Vacuum Cleaner

Nov 2017

Final instrumentation and signal processing project. We designed a self-steered robotic vacuum cleaner that with the input of 3 ultrasolic sensors was aware of objects and walls, thus changing its direction when needed.
